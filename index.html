Human Breathalyzer To monitor Ketosis.

A tool to monitor the presence of acetone in our breath. It uses gas sensor MQ-135, 1.3inch OLED display, DHT22 & Arduino Uno. Measuring acetone in breath can provide a non-invasive way to monitor the levels of ketones in the body.

3D-Gyro Simulation to control orientation of virtual object.

Arduino Nano & MPU-6050 takes real-time motion data and controls an orientation of virtual 3D cube. By bridging the physical and virtual realms, it provides an experience of interacting with a gyroscope-like object.

Getting Fire Alert Messages on Telegram.

Using Esp32 and Flame sensor (IR-sensor) we can get fire hazard alerts and notification in our smart phones. Telegram messaging app works better for it.Flame or a light source of a wavelength in the range of 760 nm to 1100 nm can be detected using IR sensor.
